About it

Why I do this?

A personal project

I started doing this personal project since I like to capture moments every time I travel to a different place to where I live, and I decided that instead of taking pictures of myself, i will take pictures of my little stuffed plushes from the One Piece series, since I like them a lot and it is a way to bring them with me everywhere.

PLACES

Places where I travel with them

Stockholm

Stockholm is a very beautiful city, full of aquatic landscape and forests wherever you look. I visit with my family the whole center of the city, through the palace and its museums like the Nobel Prize one, and the Viking.

See more about the city

Barcelona

Here I visit the basilica of the Sagrada Familia, La Casa Batlló, the Cathedral of Barcelona and the Gothic neighborhood of the city. What I liked more here was the originallity of the architecture that the architect Antoni Gaudi created in the city.

See more about the city

Copenhague

Copenhague is a very beautiful city! Although the weather was always gray, you can see very well the landscapes offered by this city. Here I visit the gardens of Tivoli, try food in Rundertarn, walk through the Amalienborg Palace and visit the famous statue of the little mermaid.

See more about the city

Oslo

Oslo is a city with a lot of culture and museums, also stands out for its aquatic landscapes and its large parks. Here I visited the monolith of the Vigeland Park, the Nordic people museum, the museum of the artist Much, and the Oslo´s Opera house.

See more about the city
